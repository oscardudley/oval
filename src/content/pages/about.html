About Us

Who We Are

About Oval Electrical

We are driven by a commitment to excellence, safety, and reliability in every project we undertake. Founded in 2023 by our director, Harry McLoughlin, our company has built a strong client base in Brighton & Hove. During this time, we have gained a reputation for thorough and friendly service.

Company director Harry brings years of experience in the electrical industry and a smile to every job. His journey from dedicated apprentice to founder of his own electrical services company is a testament to his expertise and commitment to service excellence.

Where We Are

Proud To Be Local

Oval Electrical is proud to serve Brighton, Hove and the surrounding areas. Our team of skilled electricians is dedicated to providing top-quality electrical services to residential and commercial clients throughout the region.

Our service area includes:

  • Brighton & Hove
  • Worthing
  • Shoreham
  • Burgess Hill
  • Uckfield
  • Lewes

Whether you're located in the heart of Brighton or in one of the nearby communities, Oval Electrical is here to meet your electrical needs. We understand the importance of prompt, reliable service, and we strive to arrive at your location on time, ready to tackle any electrical challenge.
