Comprehensive Safety Certificates

  • Electrical Installation Condition Report (EICR)

    This report assesses the condition of an existing electrical installation against BS 7671 safety standards. It identifies any deficiencies, potential risks, or non-compliances with the current regulations. EICRs are essential for landlords, homeowners, and commercial property owners to ensure their properties are safe and compliant.

  • Portable Appliance Testing (PAT) Report

    PAT testing involves inspecting and testing all portable electrical appliances to ensure they are safe to use.

  • Minor Electrical Installation Works Certificate (MEIWC)

    This certificate is issued for minor electrical works that do not extend or alter existing circuits significantly.

  • Electrical Installation Certificate (EIC)

    An EIC is provided following the installation of a new electrical circuit, or when significant alterations or additions are made to existing circuits.

  • Fire Alarm System Certificate

    This report certifies that a fire alarm system is installed correctly and operates according to the relevant British Standards.

  • Emergency Lighting Completion Certificate

    This certificate is issued upon the installation or inspection of emergency lighting systems, ensuring that they meet the necessary safety standards and regulations.
