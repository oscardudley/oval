Why Choose Oval?

At Oval Electrical, we're not just about wiring and circuits; we're about delivering an electrifying experience that leaves our clients truly satisfied. Our company is built on a foundation of trust, expertise, and dedication to providing top-notch electrical services to homeowners in Brighton and West Sussex.

Whether you need an electrical safety check, want to upgrade your lighting, or require a complete rewiring, Oval Electrical is your trusted partner in creating a safer and more efficient home environment.
